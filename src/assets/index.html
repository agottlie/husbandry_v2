About

Borne of a desire to provide a soundtrack for the apocalypse-that-never-was in 2012, Husbandry blends prog-tinged, churning post-hardcore riffs and soaring, soulful vocals for what can only be described as the war cry of a team of genetically engineered future warriors riding into battle on the backs of robotic behemoths. It’s pretty, pretty good.

Carina Zachary - vocals, Bronx street cred

Jordan Usatch - guitars, Seinfeld references

Arnau Bosc - bass, vocals, hilarious mispronunciations

Andrew Gottlieb - drums, food recommendations
